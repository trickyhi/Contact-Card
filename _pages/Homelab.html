Access Instructions

Step One

Create a plex account through plex.tv. There are also options sign up using your Google or Facebook account. (You don't need to buy a Plex Pass)

Step Two

Go to your profile and send either your username or email to me

Step Three

Wait for me to add you as a friend. You should receive an email, follow the directions in the email and accept the invitation.

Step Four

You're all set you can watch movies through the web player at plex.tv or use Xbox, Smart TV, etc. (iOS requires a $5 payment). Feel free to make requests for new content through The Request Form, you can use your plex username.
